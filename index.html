Our Location - Bangkok University

How to Get Here

Bangkok University is conveniently located and accessible by multiple modes of transportation:

  • By Car: Easily accessible via the main expressways. Parking is available on campus.
  • By BTS Skytrain: Get off at Ekkamai station, and take a taxi to the campus (approximately 10 minutes).
  • By Bus: Several bus routes pass near the university. Bus numbers include 25, 72, and 149.
  • By Taxi: Taxi services are readily available and can bring you directly to the campus.
